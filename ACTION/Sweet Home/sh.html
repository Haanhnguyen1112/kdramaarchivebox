Summary

Sweet Home tells the story of a young man, Cha Hyun-soo, moving into an apartment after the tragic loss of his family from a car accident. Soon after, monsters begin to invade and wipe out humanity, as survivors and residents trapped inside the apartment building find ways to survive as long as they can.
